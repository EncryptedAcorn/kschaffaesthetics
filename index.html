About

Hi, I’m Kaitlyn Schaff, BSN, RN—a registered nurse with years of experience in acute care and a passion for aesthetics. My background spans Cardiac Telemetry, Med-Surg/Oncology, and Neuro nursing, where I developed advanced skills in patient care, precision, and safety. As a certified nurse injector, I bring the same level of clinical excellence to the world of aesthetics. I specialize in Botox, dermal fillers, and lip enhancements, always focusing on creating natural, confidence-boosting results tailored to each client’s unique features. My approach is grounded in both artistry and evidence-based practice—I combine a trained clinical eye with a love for helping others feel their best inside and out. Whether you’re seeking subtle rejuvenation or a refreshed look, my goal is to provide a safe, comfortable, and empowering experience for everyone. If you’ve read this far I just wanna say THANK YOU!
